Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

Delicious Chinese-style chicken and broccoli stir fry.

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 4 large boneless chicken breast
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 tablesopoon instant chicken bouillon granules
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger, or more to taste
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  1. Combine soy sauce and 1 tablespoon cornstarch in a glass bowl or zip-top bag. Add chicken; stir to coat. Remove all air from baggie before sealing, or cover the bowl.
  2. Combine boiling water and bouillon granules in another bowl, stir until dissolved. Add remaining cornstarch and ginger; stir to combine.
  3. Heat half of the canola oil and 1 tablespoon sesame oil to 365 degrees F, in an electric skillet or in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Add broccoli, bell peppers, onion and garlic.
  4. Heat remaining canola oil and sesame oil in same skillet to 365 degrees F or medium-high heat. Add chicken mixture and crushed red pepper. Stir fry until chicken is no loinger pink in the centers and juices run clear.
